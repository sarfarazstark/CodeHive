ABOUT US

Since 1961, Dada Boudi Restaurant has been a culinary cornerstone in Barrackpore, Kolkata, celebrated for its delicious Mughlai cuisine, especially biryani and kebabs. With three outlets, we pride ourselves on offering a family-friendly atmosphere, reasonable prices, and unforgettable dining experiences.
